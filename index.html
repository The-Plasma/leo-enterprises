About Rig Expert

Rig Expert Ukraine Ltd. is a leading developer and manufacturer of antenna analyzers, transceiver interfaces, and amateur radio software. Since then the RigExpert has become a recognized brand Worldwide. Radio amateurs all over the World associate the RigExpert brand with high quality, reliability, and functionality.

POWERR SUPPLY P500

The Shackmaster Power 500 is a compact, high-performance power supply with a 36A/13.8V output, designed for demanding applications. It features low acoustic noise, real-time monitoring via a multi-function display, intelligent protection, and operates in extreme temperatures from -30°C to +70°C, ensuring reliable power with maximum efficiency and minimal heat.

Fobos SDR

The Fobos SDR is a versatile radio receiver that lets you tune into frequencies from 0.1 to 6000 MHz, customize signal processing, and update features via software without needing extra hardware. It offers endless possibilities for exploring and analyzing radio signals.

Amplifier

The RigExpert 2.4GPA is a compact yet powerful bidirectional amplifier designed for signal boosting in UAVs, ham radio, IoT, and measurement applications. It guarantees reliable performance with conformance to its stated specifications.

AA-55

The RigExpert AA-55 ZOOM is a portable, factory-calibrated analyzer for HF systems up to 55 MHz. Its large screen with ZOOM function and features like SWR2Air and multiSWR modes allow for precise measurements and easy tuning.

Stick Xpro

The RigExpert Stick XPRO is a portable, factory-calibrated analyzer for UHF systems up to 1000 MHz, designed for on-the-go use. With up to 16 hours of battery life and a bright, sunlight-readable display, it’s perfect for fieldwork and outdoor environments.

AA-650

The RigExpert AA-650 ZOOM is a high-performance analyzer for UHF systems up to 650 MHz, offering precise calibration and advanced features. Its large screen and ZOOM function allow you to explore every detail of your measurements.
